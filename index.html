About

I'm Sean Kelly, a consultant providing professional services in Python, specializing in data science, big data, large language models, audio/video, content management systems,and more.

Where possible, I both leverage open source software and make my work available as open source software. I'm a member of the Apache Software Foundation and former Vice President of the same. I'm also a supporting member of the Python Software Foundation.

When I'm not coding, I'm an amateur gastronome and former mixologist. I also like to tell jokes. Some of them are original. But the funny ones are shamelessly stolen from others.
